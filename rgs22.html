Our Mission

Where Purpose
Meets Potential

RGS Academy was founded by Sanjeev Choudhary (Sonu RGS) with a singular vision — to transform the lives of rural youth through the discipline, camaraderie, and values that sport instills. Based at RGS Academy Ground No. 2 in Narmdapuram district, we believe every child deserves a chance to discover their greatness.

Our work goes beyond athletics. We build resilient, confident young people who carry the lessons of the field into every aspect of their lives — becoming leaders in their communities and champions in their hearts.
